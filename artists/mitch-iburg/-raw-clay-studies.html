Raw Clay Studies
Mitch Iburg — 2022
sold
Raw clays and minerals, unfired. Forty unfired spheres made from different unprocessed clays. This installation is intended to bring viewers into the moment of first discovering the properties of a new found material. Variations in color, texture, stone impurities, and organic matter are brought out by carefully washing and paddling the spheres multiple times through the drying process.
