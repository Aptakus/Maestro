The Ancient Oceans

To mark the opening of its first exhibition, Sage Culture developed the short film, The Ancient Oceans. Shot in Cornwall, UK, it reinforces the gallery's concept and transmits its intent to highlight the work of artists who are deeply inspired by nature as their primary source of inspiration.
