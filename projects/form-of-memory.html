Form of Memory

On the occasion of Mitch Iburg's solo exhibition entitled, Form of Memory, Sage Culture developed a short film and a photographic series depicting the ceramist working process. The short documentary has been selected for the 2019 Minneapolis Saint Paul and Chicago International Film Festivals.

Short Documentary

Sage Culture collaborated with Mitch Iburg in a very open process. The primary purpose was to apprehend the artist's universe, showing his influences and working trajectory. To achieve this goal, Sage embarked on a journey from California to Minnesota, both significant places for Mitch and the development of his own artistic identity.

Both the film and photo series provide insight into the roles of place, material, and philosophy in defining the works created for the exhibition.
